Carolyn Abott

Associate Professor of Political Science

Baruch College, City University of New York

Carolyn Abott is a political scientist whose research focuses on American political institutions, state and local governance, public finance, and interest group politics. Her book The Politics of Public Pensions (Columbia University Press, 2025) examines how state-level party politics shapes public sector pension policy. She is currently working on a second book on interest groups and democratic inequality in one-party cities.
